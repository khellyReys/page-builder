Aman New York
Occupying the iconic Crown Building at the corner of Fifth Avenue and 57th Street, Aman New York is an ultra-luxury urban sanctuary in the heart of Manhattan. The property features 83 suites and 22 branded residences with soaring ceilings, working fireplaces, and bespoke Japanese-inspired interiors. Guests enjoy a three-story 25,000 sq ft spa and wellness center, a jazz-era lounge, world-class Italian dining at Arva, and panoramic views stretching from Central Park to the city skyline.
